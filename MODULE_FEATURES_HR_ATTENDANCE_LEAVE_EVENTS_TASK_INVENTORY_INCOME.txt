HR AND PAYROLL MODULE FEATURES
================================
1. Employee management
- Add Employee form with complete profile capture.
- Employee List with search/filter style listing and KPI cards.
- Employee records editing and lifecycle management support.
- Employee account/profile linkage with role/position-based access logic.

2. Department management
- Add new department.
- Department list with employee count per department.
- Department update and delete operations.
- Department actions controlled by permission level (full vs view).

3. Position and access matrix management
- Add position with department mapping.
- Position list with permission summary.
- Section and subsection permission assignment per position.
- Permission legend and per-module access mode (Full Access, View Only, No Access).

4. Payhead management
- Payhead catalog/listing interface.
- Earning, deduction, and other type grouping.
- Active/inactive status management for payheads.
- Search/filter support for payhead records.

5. Payroll processing
- Payroll page with KPI metrics and payroll controls.
- Employee-wise payroll rows with editable fields and calculations.
- Status tracking (pending/paid/hold/processed style states).
- Payroll actions for processing and operational handling.

6. Salary sheet and payslip
- Salary Sheet view with filters, KPI cards, and tabular salary data.
- Structured salary table with totals and summary/footer calculations.
- Payslip page support for employee payslip display.

7. Resignation and offboarding
- Resign Rule management interface with filters and status handling.
- Resignation workflow page with statuses (submitted, approved, rejected, relieved, cancelled).
- Resignation list, review, and action capability.

8. Rejoin support
- Rejoin page routing/flow available in module navigation (currently redirects path).
